“Book Now”

Display a catalog of available vehicles with:
Car image

Price range
Car type (economy, SUV, luxury)
Transmission type

Pickup location input

“Weekend Deals”
“Long-term Rental Discounts”

Admin panel to manage cars and bookings
Email confirmation after booking

🌟 Key Features & Implementation:

  • Elite Fleet Catalog: A dynamic, searchable gallery featuring luxury SUVs like the Range Rover Sport and reliable economy models like the Toyota Corolla. Includes real-time filtering by body type and transmission.

  • Intelligent Booking Engine: A multi-step reservation system that captures trip specifications, driver details, and payment preferences (MTN/Vodafone Mobile Money or Card) with an instant digital receipt.

  • Technical Design Recipe:

    • Typography: Bold, high-energy Space Grotesk headlines paired with a clean Inter body font.

  • Conversion-Optimized Layout: A sticky navigation bar with a prominent "Book Now" action and a quick-booking hero bar for desktop users.

  • Home: High-impact hero section, featured fleet, and "Why Choose Us" proof points.
